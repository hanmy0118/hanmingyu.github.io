AWESOME PRODUCTS

Team Incepxion aim to encourage office workers to stand up and move around to reduce the potential health risk. Hence we create an interactive installation named Breakfree system. Here is a just a small collection of the final products.

The poster introduces the basic concept of Breakfree system and targets of this project. We used some bright color but in strong contrast like red and blue on this poster to attract audience’s eye.

Poster

The flyer follows the same design style as the poster and same color scheme. We prepare 25 flyers for users to take away. On the flyer, we talked about the dangers of sedentary behavior, how to interact with the Breakfree system.

Flyer

The final installation of the Breakfree system is quite simple. We user Kinect to detect user’s body movement and transmit the data into the laptop, and then project into the screen. The user could stand in front of the screen and interact with the game.

Installation

In the video, we use vivid animation to visualize the boring researched data to popularize the dangers of sedentary behavior, as well as introduce the design concept and how to interact with the system in a lively way.
